Quick and easy 2 pot lasagna

First you're gonna need a full cow carcass to harvest the meat off.

Second you're going to want to have at least 10 euros on-hand to pay the bartenders.

Ingredients

  • Whole cow
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Blood of the lamb
  • Monkey's paw

Steps

  1. Boil the blood of the lamb with two parts water 1 part vinegar
  2. Age the cow for 69 days in walk-in wardrobe
  3. Eat cheddar cheese
  4. Enjoy cow lasagna
