Chicken Fajitas!

Home

Sizzling hot and bursting with flavor, chicken fajitas are a Tex-Mex favorite that bring together tender strips of marinated chicken, colorful bell peppers, and onions, all seared to perfection in a hot skillet. Seasoned with a bold blend of smoky cumin, zesty lime, and a touch of chili, each bite delivers the perfect balance of spice and freshness. Wrapped in warm flour tortillas and topped with creamy guacamole, tangy sour cream, and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, these fajitas make for a mouthwatering, crowd-pleasing meal. Ingredients

  • 600g chicken thigh (or breasts, but it will be less juicy)
  • 3 kinds of bell peppers (all different colours, yellow, red, green)
  • 1 paprika

Steps

  1. Make the marinade
  2. Marinade the chicken in the marinade
  3. Cool chicken under the grill setting at 250 or as high as your oven will go
