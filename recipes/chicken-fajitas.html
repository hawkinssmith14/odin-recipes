Sizzling hot and bursting with flavor, chicken fajitas are a Tex-Mex favorite that bring together tender strips of marinated chicken, colorful bell peppers, and onions, all seared to perfection in a hot skillet. Seasoned with a bold blend of smoky cumin, zesty lime, and a touch of chili, each bite delivers the perfect balance of spice and freshness. Wrapped in warm flour tortillas and topped with creamy guacamole, tangy sour cream, and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, these fajitas make for a mouthwatering, crowd-pleasing meal.
